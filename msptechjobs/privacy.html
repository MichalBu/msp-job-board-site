Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-12

1. What we collect

  • Interest form: company name, role, budget, and email address you submit voluntarily.
  • Checkout: handled entirely by Paddle — we receive your name, email, and order details, but never your full card number (Paddle processes and stores payment details as merchant of record).

2. How we use it

To fulfill your job listing order (posting it into the relevant community channel), to contact you about your order or listing, and — if you used the interest form — to follow up about the service. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Who we share it with

  • Paddle.com Market Ltd — payment processing and merchant-of-record billing.
  • FormSubmit.co — processes the interest-capture form submissions.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website.

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing the data they process on our behalf.

4. Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you at any time by emailing team@atlasventuresgroup.net.

5. Changes

We may update this policy as the service evolves during beta. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
