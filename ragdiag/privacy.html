Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-22

1. What we collect

  • Free-audit / contact form: your work email, and — optionally — a description of your RAG stack and the problem you're seeing.
  • Audit data you choose to send us: sample queries, retrieved context, and failure examples, used only to run your diagnostic.
  • Checkout: handled entirely by Paddle — we receive your name, email, and order details, but never your full card number (Paddle processes and stores payment details as merchant of record).

2. How we use it

To run and return your diagnostic, to contact you about your audit or subscription, and to provide the service. The failure data you send is used only to produce your report and follow up with you. We do not sell your data, and we do not share your audit data with other customers.

3. Who we share it with

  • Paddle.com Market Ltd — payment processing and merchant-of-record billing.
  • FormSubmit.co — processes contact/free-audit form submissions.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website.

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing the data they process on our behalf.

4. Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you at any time by emailing team@atlasventuresgroup.net.

5. Changes

We may update this policy as the service evolves during early access. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
