Refund / Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-22

14-day money-back guarantee

You may request a full refund within 14 days of any charge, no questions asked. Just email team@atlasventuresgroup.net with the email address used at checkout and your order reference.

Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel at any time to stop all future renewals. Email team@atlasventuresgroup.net and we'll confirm the cancellation; you keep access through the end of the period you've already paid for.

How refunds are processed

Refunds are processed by Paddle, our merchant of record, and typically appear back on your original payment method within 5–10 business days.
